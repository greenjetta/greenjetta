Tribute to Betty White

Betty White was a beloved national treasure.

Not only was she beloved for her kind spirit,

but praised for her amazing accomplishments.


Betty was a:


  • Eight time Emmy Winner,
  • Three time Screen Actors Guild Award Winner,
  • Three time American Comedy Award Winner,
  • Grammy Award Winner.
